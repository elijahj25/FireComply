The Problem

Compliance shouldn't mean paperwork chaos.

Most restaurants still manage hood cleaning records with paper binders, email chains, and spreadsheets. That's a fire inspector's nightmare — and yours.

📁

Paper records get lost

Physical binders go missing, get damaged, or sit in a manager's car trunk. When the fire inspector arrives, you're scrambling.

📅

Services fall through the cracks

No one remembers when the last service was done. Quarterly intervals turn into six months. Suddenly you're overdue — and non-compliant.

📸

No photo documentation

Before/after photos end up in a technician's camera roll or never taken at all. You have no proof of the work that was done.

🔧

Deficiencies slip through

Issues get noted verbally and forgotten. Grease buildup, worn belts, broken latches — they become serious hazards when untracked.
